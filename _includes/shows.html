Couch Surf

A night of improv comedy from Glasgow Improv Theatre's Couch and special guests who are crashing for the night.

No two nights are the same as our rotating cast of some of the best improvisers in the country take the true stories of our special guests and turn them into sketches, made-up on the spot.

Past guests have included Marc Jennings (Scottish Comedian of the Year), Stuart McPherson (BBC's Scot Squad), Toni Charline (Comedy Bang! Bang! & UCB) and Patrick Noth (MTV & Comedy Central & UCB).

1st + 3rd Saturdays of month

Blackfriars Basement (Glasgow)

Tickets: Couch Surf Eventbrite Tickets
