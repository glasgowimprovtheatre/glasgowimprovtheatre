Sketch writing

The GIT+The Sketch School Present:

Start Writing Sketch Comedy Now

Taught by Marc Warzecha


The Glasgow Improv Theatre is excited to partner with The Sketch School to bring a sketch comedy course to and specifically for Glasgow. Start Writing Sketch Comedy Now is taught by the Award Winning Marc Warzecha who was the former Associate Artistic Director of The Second City and has written for The Detroiters.


This 5-week course will focus on:

  • Essentials of sketch comedy structure
  • Tips and tricks to generate useful sketch ideas quickly
  • How to find and heighten “game” in your sketch
  • An overview of some of the most popular different types of sketches
  • Repeatable methods to continue to create your own sketches in your own comic voice

This course will run on Zoom on Thursdays from 7- 9:30 PM, Apr 29th - May 27th.
